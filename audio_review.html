What this review covers

The recording, Zero Days to SQLi, distills Lesson 8 into an applied narrative — moving from initial discovery of an unknown flaw to full vulnerability prioritization and mitigation. It reinforces the entire vulnerability management lifecycle:

  • Zero-day analysis: how defenders triage brand-new vulnerabilities using limited intelligence and proof-of-concept data.
  • Threat intelligence fusion: correlating OSINT, proprietary feeds, and community reports to determine exploit maturity and likelihood.
  • CVSS scoring in context: adjusting base scores with temporal and environmental factors to reflect real-world business impact.
  • Exploitation path mapping: linking critical flaws (like SQLi) to affected assets, exposure levels, and potential data sensitivity.
  • Remediation strategy: balancing patch urgency, compensating controls, and operational uptime while verifying via re-scan.
  • Continuous improvement: feeding verified findings back into SBOM management and vulnerability intelligence workflows.
